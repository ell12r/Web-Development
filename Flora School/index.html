Frequently asked questions

  • How to use the school app?
    Navigate to the School App page and download the preferred file (android or Windows). Install the app on your device. Use the fees tab to know your outstanding fees and make payments. You can pay daily feeding fee using the app by opening the canteen dialog and then choosing feeding fee.
  • How to get logins for the school app?
    Send an email to the IT Department containing Your name, the name of your ward(s) and the latest reciept of fees paid. A link will be sent to you, create your username and Password using the link. You can now login to your school app with the information you signed up with. Visit the school for any assistance
